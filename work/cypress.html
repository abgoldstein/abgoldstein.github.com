the elevator pitch

Cypress is an open source testing tool that can be used to verify the accurate calculation of the Stage 1 Meaningful Use Clinical Quality Measures. The Cypress project is being stewarded by The MITRE Corporation and is being funded by the Office of the National Coordinator for Health Information Technology (ONC) within the United States Department of Health and Human Services (HHS). Cypress will undergo the processes mandated by regulation for all persons or entities that submit a test tool and/or procedure to the National Coordinator to be considered for approval for use in testing and certification in the ONC EHR Certification Programs.
